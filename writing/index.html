Emotion vs Thought

october 22, 2023

Everyone is born with a level of emotion and a level of thoughts. These two battle each other over whether someone lives more in their mind (thoughts) vs in the present and in reality (emotions).

When people overthink and get stuck in their thoughts they become trapped (or stuck in the mud) and unable to act. On the flip side, when one gets overtaken by powerful emotions they act out in an irrational way without thinking about it. Everyone is born with an innate mixture of these and lives accordingly. Having a good balance is key to truly living, acting thoughtfully, in the world.

And to really thrive you have to let thought and emotion work together to enter a flow state and let yourself go.

Live with eyes open

june 26, 2023

I naturally live through thinking. I see the outline of what’s around me but my mind predicts all the details. My mind bases its predictions on its knowledge and past experience. The problem with this is that everything is what I expect it to be and I don’t notice the beauty in the detail and subtle differences. When you start to let your eyes perceive before your brain you notice things you’ve never seen before and the world around us becomes infinitely more interesting and engaging. I want to start living consciously — and through my eyes instead of my brain.

Close your eyes. Take deep breaths. Inhale, exhale, inhale, exhale. Feel your body relax and your mind zoom in on the breath like a telescope. When your mind is clear of past thoughts, accept the refreshed perspective. Your eyelids, dragging in opposite directions, open and you start to live through your sight. Focusing on the present moment you admire the beauty in life and feel the corners of your mouth pull up and away into an enchanting world. Embrace living by accepting nature’s aroma, expressing yourself through art, and questioning the unknown. I want to teach Conscious Living Through Appreciation.

Writing

I love writing because it helps me to slow my thinking down and lets me explore and develop my thoughts. When I’m just thinking, my head jumps around so fast between thoughts that it’s hard for me to dig deeper beyond a thought’s surface. I’m always thinking about so much at once, and just focusing on breathing helps me to keep my mind in one place and stay in the present moment. Similarly, writing helps me keep my mind in one place and focus on one thing. Writing and journaling can be a very powerful method to help me relax and slow down in life.

When I’m in my writing flow state I am fully in tune with my present thought. I am in a state of pure focus and am without distractions.

I can try to have the same result I get from writing from speaking. Because in a way they are the same. By speaking slowly and enunciating I can slow my thoughts and mind down and live in the present.

Community Fulfillment

On earth every person has different physical and mental attributes. I think everyone has these attributes for certain reasons — we all are the way we are for a reason. Everyone’s own attributes are meant for them to do a certain task for the world. Everyone is different, and so we, the people of the world, must work together and complete our roles for the community of humanity. When everyone performs their roles when needed, we can fulfill and complete our community.

When everyone does what they were built to do for the entire community, the world becomes more communal and much more efficient. If everyone does their tasks for the benefit of the community we can complete each other.

For example, I am tall, so I was meant to reach tall things. So whenever someone is trying to get something high up, it is my duty in the community to help them out and get it. On the flip side, whenever there is something in a small congested space it is a smaller person’s duty to complete the task.

Everyone is a unique piece of a puzzle, which when complete is a community of humans in perfect harmony.
